## 0.1.0

* Initial release.
* `Loupe` — bare widget wrapping `RawMagnifier` with full `ShapeBorder` support
  (circle, rounded rectangle, stadium, star, custom).
* `LoupeController` — `ChangeNotifier` for programmatic show/hide/move.
* `DraggableLoupe` — plug-and-play wrapper with built-in gesture handling,
  smooth scale animation, optional external controller, and long-press mode.
